Planning Tips

Room Allocation

  • Consider room capacity when assigning sessions
  • Leave buffer rooms for technical issues or overflow
  • Group similar session types in nearby rooms

Scheduling

  • Allow 15-30 minutes between sessions for transitions
  • Schedule high-attendance sessions in larger rooms
  • Consider parallel sessions to maximize attendance
